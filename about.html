About Kalvimini

Empowering Tamil Nadu students with free, quality study materials

Who We Are

Kalvimini is a free educational platform dedicated to helping 10th and 12th standard students of Tamil Nadu Board score better in their board examinations.

We provide important questions, chapter-wise notes, model question papers, previous year papers, and the latest exam news — all completely free.

Our content is carefully prepared based on the latest Tamil Nadu State Board syllabus and exam patterns to ensure students get the most relevant study materials.

Whether you are studying in Tamil medium or English medium, Kalvimini has resources for you.

Our Mission

Every student deserves access to quality education regardless of their financial background. Kalvimini was created with one goal — to make the best study materials freely available to every Tamil Nadu student. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no hidden fees.
