About KalviMini Tuition

Your trusted free study resource for Class 6–12 students following the Tamil Nadu State Board curriculum.

Who We Are

Bringing Quality Education to Every Student

KalviMini Tuition is a free online educational platform dedicated to Tamil Nadu State Board students from Class 6 to Class 12. We believe that every student — regardless of location or background — deserves access to well-structured, exam-focused study materials in their own language and curriculum.

Our platform is built by experienced educators who understand the exact needs of Tamil Nadu Board students: clear notes, important questions, model papers, and original question papers with answer keys — all in one place, all for free.
